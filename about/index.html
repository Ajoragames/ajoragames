Who We Are

About Ajora

An independent studio building browser games that hook you in seconds — no downloads, no accounts, just a link and a high score to chase.

Ajora Games is an independent gaming studio focused on one thing: making games that are impossible to put down.

We build exclusively for the browser. No installs, no friction — just a link and a game that hooks you in seconds. Fast, emotional, replayable.

What started as a single game has grown into a full arcade. Original concepts like Last One Standing and Would You Rather Ranked now sit alongside genre classics — Chess, Solitaire, Tetris — and daily games like Word Streak, built to bring you back every single day.

The Manifesto

“We believe the best games don't need big budgets. They need great concepts, emotional moments, and relentless replayability.”
