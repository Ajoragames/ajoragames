Privacy Policy

What we collect, why we collect it, and how advertising works on this site.

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Ajora Games ("we", "us") operates ajoragames.com (the "Site"), a free-to-play browser arcade. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit or play, and how it's used. Playing our games doesn't require an account for most titles, and we aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

Information We Collect

Depending on the game you play, we may collect:

  • Gameplay data — scores, progress, and in-game stats, stored via our backend (Supabase) to power leaderboards, achievements, and save states.
  • Usage data — pages visited, device/browser type, and approximate location (derived from IP), collected automatically for analytics and site performance.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — small files stored in your browser to remember preferences and support advertising, described below.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. This Site is not directed at children, and if we learn a child has provided personal information, we will delete it.

Advertising & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads and help fund development of the Site and its games. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendor cookies via the Digital Advertising Alliance.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

If you are located in the European Economic Area or UK, ads on this Site are shown only after you provide consent through our cookie consent tool, in line with Google's EU User Consent Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google AdSense — advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — backend infrastructure for scores, achievements, and (where applicable) in-app purchases.
  • Cloudflare — hosting and content delivery.

Your Choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking cookies may affect how ads are shown and may disable some game features like saved progress or leaderboards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Site grows. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to ajoragames@gmail.com.
